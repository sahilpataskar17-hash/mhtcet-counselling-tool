About Us

MHT CET Counselling Tool is an educational website created to help students estimate their MHT-CET performance and explore college options easily.

Our platform provides tools such as:

  • MHT-CET Marks Calculator
  • Percentile & Rank Estimation
  • College Cutoff Viewer
  • College Comparison & Suggestions

All calculations and suggestions are based on previous year data, public information, and trends. This website is intended only for guidance purposes.

We are not affiliated with the State CET Cell or any official examination body.

Our goal is to make counselling information simple, accessible, and student-friendly.
